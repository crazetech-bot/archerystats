1. Introduction

The Archery Stats Management System is a web-based platform for managing archers, coaches, clubs, and performance data. It supports multi-role access — super administrators, club administrators, coaches, and archers each have a tailored experience suited to their needs.

Key features:

  • Archer and coach profile management with photo upload
  • Live scorecard entry with WA-compliant round types
  • Performance analytics with trend charts and comparison tools
  • Club management with performance dashboard and member invitations
  • Personal achievement records for each archer
  • Multi-role access control (Super Admin, Club Admin, Coach, Archer)
  • Admin-controlled registration suspension per module

2. User Roles

RoleDescription
Super Admin Full access to all modules. Can create/delete archers, coaches, clubs, and manage system settings.
Club Admin Manages their own club — can view/edit club info, invite/remove archers and coaches, and view the club performance dashboard.
Coach Can view assigned archers, log training sessions, and view performance data for their archers.
Archer Can view and edit their own profile, enter scores, and manage their personal achievements.
Default admin login: admin@archery.my / password

3. Getting Started

3.1 Registration

New users can self-register at /register. Three account types are available:

Archer

  • Select the Archer card
  • Fill in: Full Name, Email, Password, Confirm Password
  • You are logged in immediately and redirected to your archer profile to complete it

Coach

  • Select the Coach card
  • Fill in: Full Name, Email, Password, Confirm Password
  • You are logged in immediately and redirected to your coach profile to complete it

Club (Club Administrator)

  • Select the Club card — a Club Name field appears (required)
  • Fill in: Club Name, Full Name, Email, Password, Confirm Password
  • A new club record is created and linked to your account automatically
No email verification is required. You are logged in and taken to your profile straight after registering.
Registration suspended? If a role button is grayed out and labelled "Suspended", the administrator has closed registration for that account type. Contact your system administrator for assistance, or ask them to create your account manually.

3.2 Login

Go to /login and enter your registered email and password. Tick Remember me to stay logged in. Upon login, you are redirected based on your role:

  • Super Admin → Archers list
  • Club Admin → Own Club Dashboard
  • Coach → Coach profile
  • Archer → Own archer profile

3.3 Forgot Password

  1. On the login page, click "Forgot password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address and submit
  3. A password reset link is sent to your email
  4. Click the link and enter a new password (minimum 8 characters)
  5. You are redirected to login with a success message

5. Module: Archers

5.1 Archer List

URL: /archers  |  Access: Super Admin, Club Admin, Coach

Displays a table of all registered archers with: Ref No, Name, Email, Gender, Division(s), State, Club, and action buttons (View / Edit / Delete). A stats bar shows Total · Male · Female · Showing counts.

5.2 Adding an Archer

Access: Super Admin, Club Admin only

  1. Click + ADD ARCHER (top-right of archer list)
  2. Fill in the required fields: Full Name, Email, Date of Birth, Gender
  3. Select Division(s) — Recurve, Compound, Barebow, etc. (multi-select)
  4. Choose Classification (required) — U12, U15, U18, or Open
  5. Optionally fill in: Club (optional), State/National Team, Equipment, Personal Best scores, Photo, Notes
  6. Click SAVE ARCHER
The system auto-generates a unique reference number (e.g. ARCH-00001).
Classification is required when creating or editing an archer profile. Choose the age-group category that matches the archer's competition class: U12, U15, U18, or Open. Club is optional and can be assigned later.

5.3 Archer Profile

URL: /archers/{id}

The profile page displays: photo & identity card, divisions & classification badges, personal information, location, equipment, personal best scores (unofficial & official), notes, recent sessions, performance summary, and personal achievements.

Action buttons (top-right of page):

  • NEW SESSION — start a new scoring session
  • EDIT — edit profile (visible to owner and admins)
  • DELETE — permanently delete (Super Admin only)

5.4 Editing Archer Information

Access: The archer themselves, Club Admin, Super Admin

  1. Open the archer profile and click EDIT
  2. Update any fields across the sections: Basic Info, Contact, Location, Equipment, Classification, Personal Best, Notes, Photo
  3. Click SAVE CHANGES
Archers can only edit their own profile. They cannot edit other archers' profiles.

5.5 Personal Achievements

Located at the bottom of the archer profile page. Displays a table of: Date | Achievement | Team | Tournament.

Adding an achievement:

  1. Click + Add Achievement (top-right of the section header)
  2. Fill in: Date (required), Achievement title (required), Team, Tournament
  3. Click SAVE ACHIEVEMENT

Removing an achievement: Click Remove on any row (a confirmation dialog appears).

Archers can manage their own achievements. Club Admins and Super Admins can manage any archer's achievements.

5.6 Performance Analytics

URL: /archers/{id}/performance

Click View Full Analytics → in the Performance Summary card to open this page.

  • Date range selector — Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, This Year, Last Year, or Custom
  • Stat cards — Sessions count, Best Score, Average Score, Hit Rate %
  • Score Trend chart — Line chart; competition sessions highlighted in amber
  • Round Type Breakdown — Bar chart comparing averages per round type
  • Competition vs Training — Grouped bar chart with counts, averages, bests
  • Sessions table — Full list with date, round, score, X count, hits, competition badge

6. Module: Coaches

6.1 Coach List

URL: /coaches  |  Access: Super Admin, Club Admin, Coach

Displays all coaches with: Ref No (e.g. COACH-00001), Name, Email, Club, Coaching Level, State, and action buttons.

6.2 Adding a Coach

Access: Super Admin, Club Admin only

  1. Click + ADD COACH
  2. Fill in: Full Name, Email (required), Date of Birth, Gender, Phone
  3. Select Coaching Level — None / Kursus Asas Kejurulatihan / Level 1 / Level 2 / Level 3
  4. Optionally fill in: Club, Team, Address, State, Country, Photo, Notes
  5. Click SAVE COACH

6.3 Coach Profile

URL: /coaches/{id}

Displays coach details, assigned archers list, and training session history. Coaches can edit their own profile via the EDIT button.

6.4 Assigning Archers to a Coach

  1. Open the coach profile
  2. Click the Archers button (or navigate to /coaches/{id}/archers)
  3. Select an archer from the dropdown and click Assign
  4. To remove, click Remove next to their name

6.5 Training Sessions

URL: /coaches/{id}/training

Coaches can log group training sessions for their assigned archers. Click + New Training Session, fill in: Date, Title, Location, Notes, participating archers, then save. Training sessions are separate from individual scoring sessions.

7. Module: Sessions & Scorecards

7.1 Starting a New Session

Access: All roles (archers can only start sessions for themselves)

  1. Go to an archer's profile and click NEW SESSION, or navigate to /archers/{id}/sessions/create
  2. Select a Round Type from the category tabs (Indoor / Outdoor / Field / 3D / Clout)
  3. Optionally override Distance (m) and Target Face (cm)
  4. Choose session type: Training or Competition
  5. Enter a Date
  6. Click Start Session
The system pre-selects the most common round type based on the archer's primary division:
Recurve → WA 70m Outdoor Recurve  ·  Compound → WA 50m Outdoor Compound  ·  Barebow → WA 50m Outdoor Barebow

7.2 Round Types

7.3 Entering Scores

The scorecard displays 6 arrows per end. Ends are grouped into sets of 6, displayed one set at a time. End totals update automatically.

  • Typing: Click an input, type the value, press Enter or Tab to advance
  • Navigation: Use ← Previous / Next → buttons to move between sets
  • Saving: Click SAVE to save the current set to the database

7.4 Scoring Systems

SystemUsed ForValid Values
StandardRecurve, Barebow, LongbowX, 10–1, M
CompoundCompound roundsX, 10–6, M  (1–5 not valid)

Invalid values are rejected with a shake animation. The hint text at the bottom of the scorecard shows the valid values for the current round.

7.5 Session History

URL: /archers/{id}/sessions

Lists all sessions in reverse chronological order with: Date, Round Type, Score, Competition badge, and a link to the scorecard.

8. Module: Clubs

8.1 Creating a Club

Method A — Self-registration (Club Admin): Go to /register, select Club, enter a Club Name and personal details. A club is automatically created and linked to the new account.

Method B — Admin creation (Super Admin):

  1. Go to Clubs in the sidebar and click + CREATE CLUB
  2. Fill in: Club Name (required), Registration No., Founded Year, Description
  3. Add contact details: Email, Phone, Website
  4. Add location: City, Full Address, State
  5. Upload a club logo (JPG/PNG/WebP, max 2MB)
  6. Toggle Active status as needed
  7. Click SAVE CLUB

8.2 Club Profile & Edit

URL: /clubs/{id}

Displays: club logo, registration number, founded year, active/inactive status, contact information, location, and quick stats (archers count, coaches count, sessions this month).

Click EDIT to update any club details. Club Admins can only edit their own club.

8.3 Club Dashboard

URL: /clubs/{id}/dashboard

Aggregated performance data for all archers in the club.

  • Date range: Last 7 Days / Last 30 Days / This Year / Last Year / Custom
  • Stat cards: Total Archers, Active Archers, Best Score, Club Average Score
  • Score Trend chart: Line chart — daily club average score
  • Top Archers Leaderboard: Horizontal bar chart — top 8 by average score
  • Archer Performance Table: Sessions, Best, Avg, Hit Rate %, link to individual analytics
  • Recent Sessions Table: Date, Archer, Round, Score, Competition badge

8.4 Managing Members

URL: /clubs/{id}/members

Two tabs: Archers and Coaches. Each tab shows current members, pending invitations, and an invite form.

Removing a member: Click Remove from Club next to any member. The association is cleared immediately — no email required.

8.5 Membership Invitations

Inviting archers/coaches requires their acceptance via email.

  1. On the Members page, select the Archers or Coaches tab
  2. Search for an archer/coach in the Invite section and select them
  3. If the person is already in another club, a transfer warning appears
  4. Click Send Invitation

What happens: An email is sent with secure Accept/Decline links (valid 7 days). Accepting updates the archer/coach's club automatically. Pending invitations can be cancelled by the club admin.

9. Module: Admin Settings

URL: /admin/settings  |  Access: Super Admin only

9.1 Registration Control

The Registration Control card at the top of the Settings page lets you suspend or re-open public self-registration independently for each module.

ModuleEffect when Suspended
ArcherThe "Archer" button on /register is grayed out and labelled "Suspended". New archer self-registration is blocked server-side.
CoachThe "Coach" button on /register is grayed out and labelled "Suspended". New coach self-registration is blocked server-side.
ClubThe "Club" button on /register is grayed out and labelled "Suspended". New club self-registration is blocked server-side.

To suspend registration for a module:

  1. Find the module row (Archer / Coach / Club) — the status badge shows Open (green) or Suspended (red)
  2. Click Suspend and confirm the dialog
  3. Status changes to Suspended immediately

To re-open registration: Click Open Registration on the suspended row and confirm.

Suspension only affects public self-registration at /register. Admins can still create archers, coaches, and clubs manually through the system regardless of this setting.

11. Frequently Asked Questions

I registered but the role button I need is grayed out.

The administrator has suspended registration for that account type. Contact your system administrator to have it re-opened, or ask them to create your account manually.

I'm an archer and I can't see the Archers list.

Archers cannot browse the full archer list. When you log in, you are taken directly to your own profile page. You can only view and edit your own information.

How do I assign a coach to an archer?

Go to the coach's profile → click the Archers button → use the dropdown to select and assign archers to that coach.

The archer's default round type is wrong when creating a session.

The system pre-selects based on the archer's first division. If no division is set, no default is applied. You can always select any round type manually by clicking the correct category tab.

I entered a score but it was rejected.

Each round type has a specific scoring system. For example, Compound rounds do not accept scores of 1–5. The valid values are displayed at the bottom of the scorecard. Refer to Section 7.4 for the full scoring table.

Can an archer be in multiple clubs?

No. Each archer belongs to at most one club at a time. If you invite an archer already in another club, a transfer warning is shown. The archer must accept the invitation for their club to change.

An invitation I sent has expired. What do I do?

Cancel the expired invitation on the Members page and send a new one. Invitations expire after 7 days.

I'm a Club Admin but I get a 403 error when accessing another club's dashboard.

Club Admins can only access their own club's pages. Attempting to access another club's URL will return a 403 Forbidden error.

How do I delete a club?

Only a Super Admin can delete a club. Go to the Clubs list, find the club, and use the Delete action. This action is irreversible.

How do I change my password?

Log out, go to the login page, and click "Forgot password?" to reset your password via email.
